137 /* The following parameters hold the values of 4096 divided by the corresponding
138 individual quantization values. These values are needed to get around
139 actually dividing the Y, Cb, and Cr values by the quantization values.
140 Instead, you can multiply by the values below, and then divide by 4096
141 to get the same result. And 4096 = 2^12, so instead of dividing, you can
142 just shift the bottom 12 bits out. This is a lossy process, as 4096/Q divided

144 the quantization process is also lossy, so the additional loss of precision is
145 negligible. To decrease the loss, you could use a larger number than 4096 to
146 get around the actual use of division. Like take 8192/Q and then divide by 8192.**/
